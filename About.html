Hello There!

I am a UX/UI designer based in Atlanta, Georgia with a passion for creating meaningful connections and experiences with user-centered design.

I was born and raised in Sarasota, Florida and later moved to Auburn, Alabama where I attended Auburn University. After a few semesters of persuing my marketing degree, I found that I have a passion for technology and business analytics.

With my educational and professional background, I have a strong understanding of time and budget constraints, as well as understanding the importance of team collaboration and constant feedback. I believe that iterations, rapid prototyping, and usability testing bring the most powerful designs. I have always had a knack for problem solving, and through UX I am able to reduce users’ issues through design.

Outside of my professional life, you can find me traveling as much as I can, reseaching skin care products, going to concerts, spending time with my family, and taking my dog Murphy for walks on the Atlanta beltline. Since most of my day to day is spent on the computer, I value time outdoors whether thats hiking, fishing, or having a cocktail on the porch!
